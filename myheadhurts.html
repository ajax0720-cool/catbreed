For all those asking about what breed their cat is, here is ...
Cat breeds are distinct groups of domesticated cats sharing,
through selective breeding, specific genetic, physical (coat, shape), and behavioral traits.

Cat breeds

Siamese Cat: Vocal and affectionate companions with striking blue eyes and a sleek coat. They love to follow their owners around and are known for being very chatty.
Ragdoll Cat: Gentle giants that go limp when held, known for their docile nature. They have beautiful blue eyes and a soft, plush coat that feels like a ragdoll.
Persian Cat: Regal and calm with luxurious long fur that requires daily grooming. They have a flat face and a sweet personality, preferring a quiet home environment.
Munchkin Cat: Playful and energetic with short legs, proving size doesn't limit fun. Despite their unique stature, they are athletic and love to run and jump.
